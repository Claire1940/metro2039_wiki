## Survival Mechanics and Environmental Hazards

The core of the Metro experience has always been "tension through limitation." In the 2026 iteration, this concept is being expanded with more layers of simulation. You aren't just managing your gas mask filters and ammunition; you are managing your presence in a world that is actively trying to kill you.

### Dynamic Weather Systems
The weather in the new Metro title is reportedly more than just a visual effect. A sudden blizzard in a frozen wasteland can reduce visibility to near zero, forcing you to rely on your compass and sound. Conversely, heavy rain in an overgrown region can wash away your scent, making it easier to hide from the **metro 2039 new mutants** that use smell to hunt.

1.  **Filter Management:** Still a staple, but now affected by environmental humidity and toxicity levels.
2.  **Weapon Degradation:** Mud, snow, and dust will jam your improvised firearms faster than before.
3.  **Thermal Regulation:** In certain regions, staying warm is as important as having bullets.

### Resource Scarcity
Ammunition remains the primary currency and tool for survival. However, the leak suggests a deeper customization system for weapons. You can scavenge parts to turn a basic pistol into a silent, long-range carbine, but every attachment adds weight and maintenance requirements. Balancing your loadout for the specific **metro 2039 new mutants** in your current region is the key to longevity.
