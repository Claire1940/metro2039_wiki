## Environmental Storytelling: The "Frozen Stories"

A standout feature discussed by the developers is the concept of "Frozen Stories." This is a refined version of environmental storytelling where the world itself speaks to the player without the need for dialogue. 4A Games aims to make every room feel lived-in, capturing a specific moment in time right before a tragedy occurred.

Follow these steps to fully experience the "Frozen Stories" in-game:
1.  **Observe the Layout:** Look for spilled items, such as tea on a table or an unfinished card game, to understand the victim's final moments.
2.  **Check for Clues:** A corpse holding an unloaded gun with shells scattered on the floor tells a story of a desperate last stand.
3.  **Search the Periphery:** Bags packed at the door suggest an attempted escape that was cut short.

This level of detail ensures that the world isn't just a backdrop for combat but a graveyard of human history that the player must navigate. For more information on the development history of these mechanics, you can visit the [official 4A Games website](https://www.4a-games.com.mt/).
