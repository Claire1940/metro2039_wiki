## Evolution of Survival Mechanics

The core of the Metro experience has always been survival. In the time since **Metro 2039 announced** its features, it has become clear that resource management will be more punishing than ever. Players will likely need to manage not just their ammunition and gas mask filters, but also the structural integrity of their gear.

In previous games, the "Military Grade Rounds" served as both a powerful ammunition type and a form of currency. It is rumored that the 2026 title will introduce a more complex bartering system involving clean water and medicine, reflecting the worsening state of the world four years after the events of the previous mainline game.

### Enhanced Crafting and Customization

The crafting system introduced in *Exodus* is expected to return with significant upgrades. Instead of just swapping out scopes or barrels, players may be able to forge entirely new "bastard" weapons from scrap metal found in the ruins.
