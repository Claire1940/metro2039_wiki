## Technical Evolution: The Jump to Unreal Engine 5

The transition to Unreal Engine 5 is perhaps the most significant technical milestone for the franchise since the original release of the series. While the old engine provided the claustrophobic shadows we loved, the new architecture allows for a level of fidelity previously impossible. Internal tests found in the leak showcase the power of **Lumen**, specifically how it handles dynamic lighting in the tunnels.

One specific test described a single flickering candle against a rusted grate, illustrating how light now bounces realistically off surfaces, heightening the atmospheric dread. But the changes aren't just visual; the gameplay systems are receiving a massive overhaul to match the new engine's capabilities.
